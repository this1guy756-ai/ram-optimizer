How it works
Simple, safe, and instant

No tricks. Here's exactly what happens when you hit Trim.

1

You click Trim

RAM Optimizer scans every running process and identifies pages of memory that haven't been touched recently.

2

Idle pages move to standby

Using Windows' built-in EmptyWorkingSet API, those pages are moved out of active RAM into a standby list — freeing physical memory instantly.

3

Your game gets the freed RAM

The newly freed memory is available for whatever needs it — your game, your stream, your render. Nothing crashes.

4

Background apps recover naturally

If a background app needs its pages back, Windows gives them back instantly. Everything keeps working — just with less waste.
